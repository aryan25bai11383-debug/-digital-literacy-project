Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

👍 Do’s
✔️ Think before posting — ensure your content is respectful and appropriate
✔️ Maintain a professional profile (good bio, clean profile picture)
✔️ Protect your privacy — adjust settings and limit personal information
✔️ Verify information before sharing (avoid spreading fake news)
✔️ Engage positively — support, appreciate, and communicate respectfully

❌ Don’ts

❌ Do not share personal details like phone number, address, or passwords
❌ Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
❌ Do not spread rumors or unverified information
❌ Avoid excessive use of social media during study/work time
❌ Do not engage in online arguments, trolling, or cyberbullying
